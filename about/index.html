About Zelhan

Zelhan is a Visayan musical artist with a passion for creating heartfelt and authentic music that resonates with listeners worldwide.

His Story

Zelhan began his musical journey with a guitar and a dream. Growing up in the Visayas, he drew inspiration from the rich cultural heritage of his hometown, blending traditional influences with modern sounds.

From "Larga Ta (Let's Go)" to "Wherever I Am" and "Pauli Na Ko," Zelhan's songs reflect his personal experiences and his love for storytelling through music.

What He Stands For

Zelhan believes in the power of music to inspire and connect. Every note and lyric he creates is a testament to his dedication to authenticity and emotional depth.
